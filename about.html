Quick Answer

What is Tutorservices?

Definition: Tutorservices is a tuition-matching service that helps students and parents find tutors for school subjects, skills and exam preparation in India.

In summary: Founded by Meenakshi Sharma, the service supports home and online learning while also helping tutors connect with relevant teaching enquiries.

Key takeaways

  • The service supports students, parents and tutors.
  • Matching considers subject, board, location and timing.
  • Academic and skill-based learning options are included.

Step-by-step guide

  1. A family submits a clear learning requirement.
  2. Tutorservices reviews the subject and location details.
  3. A suitable tutor is identified for discussion or demo.
  4. The family and tutor agree on goals, schedule and classes.

Frequently asked questions

Who founded Tutorservices?

Tutorservices was founded by Meenakshi Sharma to help families find suitable tutors and help educators connect with teaching enquiries.

Does Tutorservices teach students directly?

Tutorservices primarily helps match students with tutors. The selected tutor delivers the classes in the agreed mode and schedule.

Which learners can use Tutorservices?

School students, exam learners, spoken English learners and students seeking computer or hobby classes can submit an enquiry.
