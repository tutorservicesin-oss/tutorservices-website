Quick Answer

What is tutor registration?

Definition: Tutor registration is the process of submitting teaching qualifications, experience, subjects and preferred locations to be considered for relevant student enquiries.

In summary: Complete and accurate tutor profiles are easier to match with students who need the same subjects, class levels and teaching mode.

Key takeaways

  • List genuine qualifications and experience.
  • Specify subjects, classes and boards clearly.
  • Keep contact and preferred-location details current.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Enter personal and contact information.
  2. Add qualifications and teaching experience.
  3. List subject expertise and preferred location.
  4. Upload the resume and submit the registration.

Frequently asked questions

Does registration guarantee student leads?

No. Registration allows Tutorservices to consider the tutor for suitable enquiries, but availability and matching are not guaranteed.

Which resume details are useful?

Include qualifications, teaching experience, subjects, class levels, boards, locations and any relevant certifications.

Can tutors teach online?

Yes. Tutors can indicate online teaching preferences along with offline or home-tuition locations.
