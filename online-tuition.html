Quick Answer

What is online tuition?

Definition: Online tuition is live tutor-led teaching delivered through video, digital whiteboards, screen sharing and online learning resources.

In summary: It removes location limits while still allowing questions, demonstrations, feedback and scheduled practice with a tutor.

Key takeaways

  • Students need a reliable device, internet connection and quiet workspace.
  • Tutor matching can extend beyond the local city.
  • Active participation and post-class practice are essential.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Choose the subject, level and learning goal.
  2. Prepare a suitable device, workspace and class materials.
  3. Join a demo and test communication and teaching tools.
  4. Follow each class with revision and assigned practice.

Frequently asked questions

Is online tuition available across India?

Yes. Online matching is not restricted by the student’s city, although tutor schedules and subject availability still apply.

What equipment is needed?

A phone, tablet or computer with stable internet, working audio and a quiet learning space is usually sufficient.

Can online tutors prepare students for exams?

Yes. Online lessons can include concept teaching, written work review, sample papers and scheduled tests.
